Sri Rama Rajyam

The Lord bears the chakra (discus) and shankha (conch), representing the destruction of ignorance and the spread of divine sound

Why I Admire Lord Hanuman

Lord Venkateswara is believed to be the manifestation of Lord Vishnu in the current age of Kaliyuga. In this dark age of spiritual decline, He descended to uplift humanity, bless sincere devotees, and offer a path to moksha (liberation) through devotion (bhakti) and surrender (sharanagati)

The Divine Qualities of Lord Hanuman

  • Known to fulfill wishes and grant prosperity to true devotees
  • Symbolizes eternal goodness, purity, and auspiciousness
  • From peace of mind to material wealth, blessings are holistic and complete
